Utanger

Your Personal Utang Tracker

Utanger is a simple app that helps you keep track of your “utangs”—the money you owe others—and the amounts they owe you. Easily log transactions, monitor balances, and stay organized without awkward follow-ups. With automatic email reminders, Utanger gently notifies people about their dues, so you can settle payments smoothly and stress-free.
